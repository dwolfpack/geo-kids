## Overview

This module takes you from "never touched an AI chat" to comfortably holding useful conversations with one. You'll meet the tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), use AI for a real task from your own week, and learn the two habits every smart AI user has: sanity-checking important answers, and keeping private information private.

Five sessions, about 15 minutes each. Do them in order — each one builds on the last. At the end there's a weekly family challenge.
